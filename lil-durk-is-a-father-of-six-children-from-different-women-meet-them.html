Lil Durk is a Father of Six Children From Different Women

Lil Durk’s kids are six in number, and they include Angelo Banks, Bella, Zayden, Du’mier, Skyler, and Willow Banks. The Chicago-born rapper once revealed that he would love to father at least ten children, and he is more than halfway to his desired goal as a father.

Born Durk Derrick Banks on October 19, 1992, the 31 year-old musician grew up without a father as his dad, Dontay Banks Sr., who is currently serving two life sentences, was incarcerated when he was only 7 months old. That put a lot of responsibilities on his shoulders from a young age, and even more so when he became a father for the first time when he was just a teenager. The rapper has since added five more kids to his brood, albeit with different women, and this is what we know about his children.

Angelo Banks Was Born When His Father Was Just A Teenager

  • Date of birth: November 29, 2011
  • Age: 12 years old
  • Mother: Nicole Covone
  • Occupation: Student

As previously indicated, Lil Dirk was just a 17-year-old teenager when he became a father for the first time. Angelo Banks is the name of that child he had more than a decade ago. Angelo was born on November 29, 2011, in Chicago, Illinois, United States.

His birth sign is Sagittarius, and he is currently 12 years old. While he is undoubtedly an American citizen by birth and parental heritage, Angelo’s ethnic identity is not as clear-cut as his national identity.

One would be hard-pressed to argue against the fact that he is predominantly of the African American ethnic group, but it would also be negligent to ignore his European ancestry of Italian from his mother’s side.

Speaking of mothers, Angelo’s father had him as a result of his relatively long-term relationship with Italian American beauty Nicole Covone. Angelo’s mother was born to Italian parents on April 25, 1990, in Chicago, Illinois, United States. Not much is known about members of her birth family except for her mother, Dinah Covone, who died in 2012.

Angelo’s Parents May Have Been High School Sweethearts

A few outlets have theorized that Angelo’s parents were high school sweethearts, but there has been no solid proof to back up that theory. We can confirm, though, that the celebrity child’s parents officially began dating in 2008, quite a while before Lil Durk became the music sensation that he is today.

It is also significant to point out that Angelo’s mother is more than two years older than his father, but the now-former couple never let something as trivial as age difference come in between them. They had Angelo three years into their relationship but ended that relationship about five years after they started dating following the birth of Angelo’s sister Bella.

The 12 year-old’s parents have both moved on with their lives and have shared custody of the two children they had together. While details of Lil Durk’s colorful relationship history can be found online, Angelo’s mum has, nonetheless, kept a low profile concerning the details of her love life, leaving us with no way of knowing who she might be romantically involved with at the moment.

Angelo Banks Has Had His Fair Share Of The Spotlight

Between having his own Instagram page where he posts pictures of himself and family members, his parents’ desire to show him off on their social media pages, and being the oldest child of one of the hottest hip-hop acts in the United States today, Angelo Banks knows all about being in the spotlight.

It is important, though, to establish the fact that his dad Lil Durk did not have as big of a musical profile as he currently does when Angelo was born. Despite his significant online presence, details of the celebrity child’s formal educational background have yet to become public knowledge.

However, one could be persuaded to agree that, given his age and family background, he is currently in the process of obtaining the required academic qualifications from some of the best schools in the United States.

  • Date of birth: July 19, 2013
  • Age: 10 years old
  • Mother: Nicole Covone
  • Occupation: Student

Bella Banks was born on July 19, 2013, and is currently 10 years old. While she was born in the United States, we have yet to ascertain the exact place of her birth in the North American country. Bella was born with the birth sign of Cancer and is an American citizen not just by birth but as a result of her parental heritage.

Bella is the youngest of the two children African American rapper Lil Durk had with his Italian-American ex-girlfriend Nicole Covone. Having an Italian American mother also means that Bella has Italian ancestry through her mother.

Bella is almost two years younger than her older brother Angelo but the not-so-negligible age difference between the siblings has not prevented them from forming a close bond which has been revealed through their respective social media profiles.

Contrary to several assertions, Bella’s parents were never married, but they carried on a five-year relationship that eventually ended a few months after the birth of Lil Durk’s first daughter. The reason for her parents’ split was never officially revealed, but many believed that Covone, Bella’s mom, had had enough of Lil Durk’s philandering ways and proceeded to call off the relationship.

Regardless, they have maintained a cordial relationship for the sake of the celebrity child and her older brother.

Lil Durk’s Oldest Daughter Owns A Beauty Brand

Details of Bella’s formal educational background have yet to be revealed, but while she is still undoubtedly in the process of achieving academic exploits, Lil Durk’s oldest daughter is already a CEO, at least if the Instagram page @bellab_beauty_ is to be believed.

The beauty brand known as Bella Beauty is responsible for creating several beauty products ranging from lip glosses to face washes. The products are mainly for women. This entrepreneurial development has invariably meant that Bella knows what it means to be in the limelight, as evidenced by the more than 16.3k followers the aforementioned Instagram page has amassed.

Her non-work-related IG handle @queen_bella_banks_, where she posts family-related content like pictures of herself, her older brother, or her parents, has also attracted more than 100k followers, and this is despite her relatively young age.

All signs point to the fact that Bella Banks will be a force to be reckoned with in the world of fashion and show business when she eventually comes of age.

The accusations of infidelity leveled at Lil Durk when he was still with Nicole Covone make a lot of sense when one considers the fact that Zayden was born just a few months after his older half-sister Bella by another woman who is not Nicole.

Zayden Banks Is Also Active On Social Media

Like his brother before him, there have been wild speculations about the identity of the woman responsible for bringing the celebrity child into this world, but the more common thread of evidence suggests that he may have been a product of his father’s relationship with Deja Monét Trimble.

Willow Banks Is Lil Durk’s Sixth Child

  • Date of birth: October 31, 2018
  • Age: 5 years old
  • Mother: India Royale
  • Occupation: Student

Willow Banks was born on October 31, 2018, in the United States. She is 5 years old, and her birth sign is Scorpio. Willow is the youngest of Lil Durk’s three daughters and also the youngest of the rapper’s six children.

Willow’s life in the spotlight has been orchestrated by both of her parents, who have massive followings across several social media platforms. However, her formal educational progression is not one of the things they often post about.
